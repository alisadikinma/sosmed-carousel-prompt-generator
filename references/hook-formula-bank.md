# Hook Formula Bank — 52 Viral Hook Templates

Adapted from Seefluencer Hook Playbook by Samuel Christ (Indonesian creator/educator). 52 battle-tested hook formulas organized by 8 psychological categories. Works for **ALL content types** — educational, tips, story, opinion, product review, affiliate, UMKM, and any carousel topic.

---

## How to Use This File

1. **Pick a psychology category** that matches your topic's strongest angle
2. **Fill the formula variables** [X, Y, Z] with your topic-specific details
3. **Check Visual Profile** below each category — key expression, lighting, camera specs
4. **Read full visual specs** from `references/hook-visual-library.md` for the selected category
5. **Pair with Visual Action Hook Bank** (hook-science.md) for the visual layer
6. **Score with Hook Scoring Gate** (hook-science.md) — must pass 3/5 before generating prompt

**Three-file workflow:** This file = **52 headline formula templates**. hook-science.md = **5 hook categories + 100 hook examples + 12 Visual Actions**. hook-visual-library.md = **deep visual specs** (expression library, lighting presets, camera angles, environment palettes, synergy matrix, anti-repetition variants).

## Category 1: "Unbeatable Value" Hooks (Price Anchoring)

**Psychology:** Disrupts price expectations through anchoring. Brain categorizes purchase as "investment" rather than expense. Presenting "hundred-thousand" price against "million-rupiah" quality triggers dopaminergic reward response for resource acquisition.

**Visual Profile:** _(Full specs: `references/hook-visual-library.md` > Unbeatable Value)_
- **Expression**: wide eyes with raised brows of genuine amazement, open smile of disbelief, one hand holding product, other palm-up "can you believe this?" gesture
- **Lighting**: butterfly 2:1, 3500K warm golden key, accent light on product surface
- **Camera**: MCU 85mm eye-level (A) | CU 50mm low angle (B) | MS 35mm high angle (C) — rotate per carousel
- **Environment**: warm lifestyle/retail, golden amber palette, product with price contrast element, quality surfaces
- **Visual Action**: Objek Absurd (primary) | Makan Nyeleneh (secondary)

| 18 | Ini gila sih, [tunjukin barang] ini **[X]** ribuan | Ini gila sih, [tunjukin barang] ini 50 ribuan |
| 19 | Tapi ini ngeri bro, lo pernah gak liat **[X]** harganya **[#]**-an | Tapi ini ngeri bro, lo pernah gak liat eyeshadow 12 warna harganya 20 ribuan |
| 35 | **[X]** ini harganya nggak sampe **[#]** | Sheet mask ini harganya nggak sampe 10 ribu |

**Psychology:** Creator signals "I've completed the search-and-filter labor for you." Saying "finally found" offers a risk-free shortcut. The "gasuka X tapi masih pengen Y" frame validates the viewer's frustration before presenting the solution.

**Visual Profile:** _(Full specs: `references/hook-visual-library.md` > Problem-Solver)_
- **Expression**: knowing empathetic gaze, one brow slightly raised, pressed lips transitioning to confident half-smile — "I had the pain, now I have the answer"
- **Lighting**: short-side Rembrandt 3:1, 3800K neutral-warm, cool subtle fill from below, faint red-amber accent
- **Camera**: MCU 85mm eye-level (A) | MCU 50mm high angle (B) | MS 35mm eye-level (C) — rotate per carousel
- **Environment**: dark minimal emerging from shadow, dark warm browns, one subtle danger-signal element
- **Visual Action**: Minum Dramatic (primary) | Contradiction Pose (secondary)

| # | Formula | Example |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Akhirnya gue nemu **[X]** yang gak gampang **[Y]** | Akhirnya gue nemu cleanser yang gak gampang bikin kulit kering ketarik |

| 26 | Kalo lo gasuka **[X]** tapi masih pengen **[Y]**, lo tinggal **[Z]** | Kalo lo gasuka makeup tebel tapi masih pengen kelihatan fresh, lo tinggal pakai lip & cheek tint ini |

## Category 4: "FOMO & Urgency" Hooks (Loss Aversion)

**Psychology:** Loss aversion is 2x more powerful than gain motivation (Kahneman). Two sub-types: **Quantity Scarcity** (inventory pressure — "jangan ampe kehabisan") and **Time Scarcity** (temporal pressure — "gue harus buru-buru"). Both trigger biological fight-or-flight response, overriding logical overthinking.

**Visual Profile:** _(Full specs: `references/hook-visual-library.md` > FOMO & Urgency)_
- **Expression**: wide alarmed eyes with brow tension, tight set jaw, head pushed forward aggressively, one hand reaching toward camera in "GRAB THIS NOW" gesture
- **Lighting**: high contrast 4:1-6:1 Rembrandt, harsh key with red-amber accent, deep urgency shadows
- **Camera**: CU 85mm eye-level (A) | MCU 50mm low angle (B) | CU extreme 135mm dutch tilt (C) — rotate per carousel
- **Environment**: dynamic disrupted with urgency cues, warm-cool clash, high contrast
- **Visual Action**: Destruction (primary) | Scale Absurd (secondary)

| # | Formula | Example |
|---|---------|---------|
| 5 | Bikin kesel aja, ini **[X]** ngeluarin **[Y]** cuma **[Z]** | Bikin kesel aja, ini brand hp ngeluarin hp flagship tapi harganya cuma 3 jutaan |
| 11 | Ini jangan ampe kehabisan lagi ya **[X]** | Ini jangan ampe kehabisan lagi ya stok limited edition-nya |
| 21 | Jangan beli **[X]** ini karena kalian bakal **[Y]** | Jangan beli moisturizer ini karena kalian bakal ketagihan teksturnya |
| 33 | **[X]** ini baru launching tapi langsung banyak yang ngikutin | Toner ini baru launching tapi langsung banyak yang ngikutin |
| 48 | Gue harus **[X]** karena lagi ada **[Y]** yang lagi berlangsung | Gue harus buru-buru karena lagi ada flash sale gadget jam segini |

**English Adaptations:**
- "Don't let [Offer/Item] run out again"
- "Don't buy [Product] because you will [Unexpected Positive Consequence]"
- "I had to rush to buy [Product] because [Scarcity Reason]"

## Category 5: "Social Proof" Hooks (Bandwagon Effect)

**Psychology:** High sales volume = mental shortcut for reliability, bypasses lengthy technical evaluation. Longevity ("udah ada sejak") signals successful navigation of market cycles. Specific numbers (not "banyak yang beli" but "300 ribu pcs") create tangible metric for quality.

**Visual Profile:** _(Full specs: `references/hook-visual-library.md` > Social Proof)_

- **Lighting**: clean butterfly 2:1, 4000K neutral-warm, even controlled fill, subtle professional rim

- **Environment**: clean professional context, neutral warmth, competence signals visible (screens, data, tools)
- **Visual Action**: Props Overflow (primary) | Extreme Close-Up (secondary)

## Category 6: "Comparison & Authority" Hooks (Status Quo Challenge)

**Psychology:** Challenges existing behavior as inferior — the "Market Disruption" play. Triggers "Am I using the wrong thing?" anxiety. "Standar terbaru yang merusak pasar" positions product as category-killer. Comparison creates cognitive dissonance between what viewer uses now and what they COULD be using.

**Visual Profile:** _(Full specs: `references/hook-visual-library.md` > Comparison & Authority)_
- **Expression**: one eyebrow raised in skeptical challenge, asymmetric smirk, chin slightly up looking down — "are you SERIOUSLY still using that?"
- **Lighting**: short-side Rembrandt 3:1, 3800K neutral-warm, red accent from side, deep shadows
- **Camera**: MCU 85mm eye-level (A) | MCU 50mm high angle (B) | MS 35mm eye-level (C) — rotate per carousel
- **Environment**: dark minimal, one subtle danger-signal or split comparison element
- **Visual Action**: Scale Absurd (primary) | Destruction (secondary)

## Category 7: "Hyper-Targeted" Hooks (Identity Callout)

**Psychology:** In-Group Bias — identifying a specific persona and their struggle creates "Cognitive Ease," making the solution feel tailor-made. High-arousal emotions (kesal/annoyed) are more likely to drive social sharing than low-arousal emotions. The **Relatability Sequence**: Identify persona → Agitate specific pain → Introduce specific solution.

**Visual Profile:** _(Full specs: `references/hook-visual-library.md` > Hyper-Targeted)_

- **Lighting**: soft loop 2:1, 3500K warm natural daylight, large soft fill wrapping face, barely visible warm rim
- **Camera**: MCU 50mm slight offset (A) | MCU 85mm high angle (B) | MS 35mm eye-level (C) — rotate per carousel
- **Environment**: authentic lifestyle setting (desk, cafe, bedroom), warm naturals, everyday recognizable objects
- **Visual Action**: Extreme Close-Up (primary) | Wrong Context (secondary)

## Psychology Trigger Summary

6 core neurological mechanisms behind all 52 hook formulas:

| Trigger | Mechanism | Hook Examples | Application |
|---------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| **Curiosity Gap** | Information deficit creates deprivation state demanding resolution | #1, #13, #46 | Lead with discovery framing, never reveal the answer in the hook |
| **Social Proof** | Mass adoption = mental shortcut for reliability | #6, #34, #43 | Cite specific numbers (units sold, star ratings, years in business) |
| **FOMO / Loss Aversion** | Pain of losing is 2x more powerful than joy of gaining (Kahneman) | #11, #48 | Quantity scarcity (stock) or time scarcity (deadline) |

## 5-Step Mega Hook Execution Checklist

Adapted from the Seefluencer "Mega Creator" framework for carousel implementation:

1. **Pinpoint Core Desire/Pain** — What does the viewer want to achieve or avoid? (e.g., "glowing skin without 10 steps")
2. **Select Psychology Entry Point** — Choose trigger from 8 categories that matches strongest product claim
3. **Fill Formula Variables** — Replace [X, Y, Z] with hyper-specific details. Use high-impact variables: exact prices (Rp25 ribuan), exact numbers (300 ribu pcs), time constraints (flash sale jam segini)
4. **Inject Credibility** — Add social proof layer: units sold, star rating, comparison to premium brand. NEVER just claim "good" — cite a specific metric
5. **Map to Visual Action** — Cross-reference with Visual Action Hook Bank (hook-science.md) for the visual layer of the carousel hook slide. The visual action IS the pattern interrupt

## Execution Rules (Do This / Not That)

| Do This | Not That |
|---------|----------|
| **High energy delivery** — match intensity of hook claim in the visual | Slow pacing — generic "product on table" image |
| **Text on screen** — hook formula rendered bold in-image (billboard-scale) | Text only in caption — 85% of carousel viewers process image first |
| **Visual proof** — if hook mentions product, show it immediately in frame | Generic B-Roll — irrelevant background during critical first impression |
| **Gen-Z language** — gue/lo, particles (sih/tuh/banget), code-mixing | Formal language — saya/kamu/anda sounds corporate, kills engagement |
| **Specific numbers** — "Rp25 ribuan", "300 ribu pcs", "bintang 5" | Vague claims — "murah", "banyak yang beli", "bagus" |
| **Show the product** — hook mentions product → product visible in image | Hide the product — makes hook feel like empty clickbait |
